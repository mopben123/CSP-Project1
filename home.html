Why do we exist?

Team 4 is a group of bright and aspiring individuals currently enrolled in an AP Computer Science Principles class. Under the mentorship of the great, masterful, godly, and all-powerful Mr. Millard, they are embarking on a journey of coding and innovation. With an insatiable thirst for knowledge and a deep passion for technology, Team 4 members have joined forces to explore the realms of computer science. Individually, they bring diverse skills and talents to the table, which, when combined, result in a formidable team. Their collective creativity knows no bounds, and they thrive on tackling complex challenges. Under Mr. Millard's expert guidance, Team 4 is dedicated to creating cutting-edge projects that not only meet the rigorous standards of their course but also push the boundaries of what's possible in the world of computer science. With Team 4's determination, Mr. Millard's guidance, and their shared enthusiasm, they are poised to make a significant mark in the field of computer science, leaving a legacy of innovation and excellence that will undoubtedly shape their futures.

“Coding is like giving a toddler a bazooka: you never know what they'll do, but they're determined to change the world one bug at a time.”
– ChatGPT

This amusing phrase emphasizes the idea that, despite the uncertain nature of their attempts, the younger generations are deeply motivated and ready to have a positive impact on the world through the medium of code. We see coding as a powerful instrument for creative problem solving. While we may face challenges and make mistakes during their combined efforts, their desire to affect significant change persists, eventually resulting in the development of ground-breaking solutions to global crises.
